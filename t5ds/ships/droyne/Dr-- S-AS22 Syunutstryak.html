Syunutstryak-class Scout/Courier (S-AS22) MCr 44.8

Owner: Droyne

Using a 100-ton streamlined hull, the Syunutstryak is a small Scout ship designed for elementary exploration and courier duties; it is typically assigned temporarily to a kroyloss. It mounts jump drive-A, maneuver drive-A, and power plant-A, giving a performance of Jump-2 and 2G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 2 parsec jump, at 9t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a computer Model1/bis. There is one six-passenger stateroom, outfitted for six Droyne; it is usable for three humans with minor alterations (equivalent to 3 standard staterooms). There are no low berths. The ship has one hardpoint; installed on the hardpoint is a T3 beam laser. There is one ship's vehicle: an air/raft. Cargo capacity is 6 tons. It has fuel scoops and refineries, as well as a Collector that can charge the jump drive.

The ship requires a crew of one: a pilot/engineer. The ship can carry a total of six Droyne.

Demand -5. Volume: 95.5 tons. MCr 44.8.
