Bretremoy-class Transport (T-KU33) MCr 334.2

Using a 1000-ton hull, the Bretremoy-class Transport is a military ship typically placed in service for a Droyne oytrip. It mounts jump drive Q, maneuver drive Q, and power plant Q, giving a performance of jump-3 and 3-G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 3 parsec jump, at 90t per parsec, and one month of operations. Adjacent to the bridge is a computer Model/3. There are two Droyne crew staterooms, a gunner squad barracks, a troop squad barracks, 24 standard passenger staterooms, and no low berths. The ship has 10 deployable single turrets armed with beam lasers. There is one ship's vehicle: a cutter. Cargo capacity is 278 tons. The ship has an unstreamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling. Its undertonnage design gives it a +1 on aerodynamic operations.

The ship requires a crew of 16: pilot, astrogator, 5 engineers, medic, 3 stewards, and 5 gunners. The ship can carry 24 high passengers.

Crew Comfort -1. Demand +1. Volume: 981 tons. MCr 334.2.
