Prospector J-BS22 Kteiroa MCr 75.5

Owner: Aslan

Using a 200-ton hull, the Aslan Kteiroa-class prospector is adapted to explorations in asteroid belts.

This particular prospector, the Staiyayu, is equipped with the Htyaoea Mark 2 jump drive, the KHAO Model B.27 maneuver drive, and the Feiweaaea B2+x power plant, and is capable of Jump-2 and 2G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports 8 weeks of operations and one jump-2. On the bridge is a computer Model/3.

There are four staterooms and four low berths. The ship has one single-turret mining laser installed. The ship carries no vehicles. Cargo capacity is 78 tons. The ship is streamlined.

The seeker requires a crew of two: pilot, and engineer/astrogator.

Volume: 199 tons
Crew comfort: +0
Passenger demand: -5
