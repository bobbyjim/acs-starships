Freighter F-ZC14 Risek MCr 539.8

Owner: Al Morai

Al Morai's World-class Freighters ply the 53 major routes of the Spinward Marches. Incapable of landing, these ships are serviced in orbit, or by their modular shuttles.

Using a 2400-ton, TL14 hull, the "Risek" mounts jump drive-Z2, maneuver drive-M, and power plant-Z2, giving a performance of jump-4 and 1G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 4 parsec jump, at 216t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/4. There are two officer's suites, 14 crew staterooms, one crew double stateroom, 8 passenger luxury suites, 8 passenger staterooms, 16 low berths. Installed weaponry include 8 T1 Missiles, 8 T1 Sandcasters, and 8 T1 Beam Lasers. Cargo capacity is 900 tons. The ship has a cluster hull.

The ship has 18 crew, and can carry 8 high, 8 middle, and 16 low passengers.
