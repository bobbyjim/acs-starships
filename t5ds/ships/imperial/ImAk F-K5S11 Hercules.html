Freighter F-K5S11 Hercules MCr 658.8

Owner: Akerut

Using a 5000-ton hull, the Hercules heavy merchant is a bulk and containerized cargo carrier developed and produced for operations in the Aramis and neighboringsubsectors by Akerut. It mounts jump drive-W, maneuver drive-W, and power plant-W, giving performance of jump-1 and 1G acceleration. On the bridge is a computer Model/2bis. There are fifteen staterooms and no low berths. The ship has four triple turrets, each mounting beam lasers. There is one ship's vehicle: a 40-ton pinnace.

The Hercules heavy merchant requires a crew of 15: pilot/captain, astrogator, 7 engineers, medic, 4 gunners, and pinnace pilot. The ship does not carry passengers.

Overtonnage: 1.5 tons
