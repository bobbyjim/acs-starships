Military Packet UM-DS43 Lone Gunman MCr 193.6

Using a 400-ton, TL12 hull, the Lone Gunman Class Military Packet is designed to conduct tactical squad operations, as well as transport VIPs securely through dangerous space.

It mounts jump drive-F, maneuver drive-H, and power plant-H, giving a performance of jump-3 and 4G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 3 parsec jump, at 40t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/3 std. There is 1 officer cabin, 5 crew staterooms, 5 spacer niches, barracks, 3 luxury passenger suites, and 12 low berths. Installed weaponry include 1 T3 Sandcaster, 1 B2 Particle Accelerator, 1 B1 DataCaster, and 1 B2 Missile. Cargo capacity is 20 tons. The ship has a streamlined hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

Carried craft include 1 Gig in a Hull Niche. The ship has 11 crew, and can carry 3 VIP passengers and 12 low passengers.
