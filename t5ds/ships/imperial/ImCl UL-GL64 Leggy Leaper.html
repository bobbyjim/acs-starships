Long Packet UL-GL64 Leggy Leaper MCr 413.5

Builder: Clan Severn
Disposition: In Service

Using a 700-ton, TL15 hull, the Leggy Leaper Class Long Packet mounts jump drive-P, maneuver drive-W, and power plant-W, giving a performance of jump-4 and 6G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports a 4 parsec jump, at 70t per parsec, and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/5 std. There are 14 staterooms and 2 low berths. Installed weaponry include 1 T3 Sandcaster, 1 T3 DataCaster, 2 T3 Missiles, and 3 T3 Beam Lasers. The ship has a 10 ton cargo hold. The ship has a lift body hull, with scoops and intakes for frontier refueling.

The ship carries 1 Gig. The ship has 8 crew, and can carry 6 luxury passengers and 2 low passengers. If needed, each luxury stateroom can bunk up to three retainers.

Overtonnage: 31 tons
Crew comfort: +3
Passenger demand: +5
