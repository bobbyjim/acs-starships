Scout/Courier S-AL20 Qratliv' MCr 145.1

Owner: Republic of Regina

Hop Scout (S-AL201), TL17, MCr 145. Using a 100-ton, TL17 hull, the Qratliv' Class Scout/Courier is designed to explore wide swaths of space in a short amount of time. It mounts maneuver drive-A, hop drive-A, and power plant-A, giving a performance of hop-1 and 2G acceleration. Fuel tankage supports one hop and one month of operations. Attached to the bridge is a Computer Model/9. There are 4 crew staterooms. Installed weaponry include 1 T3 Hybrid L-S-M. A black globe for defense is bolted into the hold. Cargo capacity is 20 tons. The ship has a lift body hull, with scoops, intakes, and bins for frontier refueling.

Carried craft include an Enclosed Air/Raft in a hull niche. The ship can hold 4 crew.

Crew comfort: +0
Passenger demand: -5
