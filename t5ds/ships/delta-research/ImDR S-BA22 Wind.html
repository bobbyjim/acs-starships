Scout/Courier S-BA22 Wind MCr 69.7

Owner: Paranoia Press, LIC

The Wind-class Extended Scout is a undertonned 200t airframe hull, designed as a larger, TL12 version of its Serpent-class Scout. The ship sports Jump and Maneuver-2. Its fuel tanks support one jump-2 and 4 weeks of operations. It has one hardpoint, with a hybrid L-M-S triple turret installed. It has one vehicle: an Air/Raft in a fitted niche.

The Extended Scout has a crew of two: Pilot/Astrogator and Gunner/Engineer. The ship can carry up to four crew members. There are 3 tons of cargo space, and one 30-ton module for mission-defined space.

Volume: 150 tons
Crew comfort: +4
Passenger demand: -5
